ABOUT ME

I love photography

Hi! I'm Ryan and I'm a computer engineering student at McMaster University, who doubles as a hobbyist photographer.

Ryan Lee


Welcome to my website. Above all, I love to create things. Whether its putting together a computer from scrap parts or curating a film documenting a service trip, my passion is to create and share. Lately I've enjoyed doing this through photography. The ability to capture moments and publish them for eternity is something that has and always will amaze me. People say a picture is worth a thousand words; I think its more than that. Life's unforgettable moments often cannot be described in words... If you'd like to join me in my adventures, feel free to send me an email via the contact section!
